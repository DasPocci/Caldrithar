Lore

Thornspeaker Vaelith

✴︎ Scene: Ash Beneath the Circle

The ritual site had been buried. Forgotten. Sealed by stone and time.
But Vaelith did not need directions.
The Weave itself had twitched.

He stood alone, robes unmoving despite the stale air. The floor beneath him was scorched in a spiral, the chalk runes long since erased — but the resonance remained.

His fingers hovered, splayed like a net.

“Thirteen signatures. Thirteen voids.”

He turned his gaze to the center of the circle — the single patch of untainted earth, untouched by arcane failure. Or success.

“And one still lingers.”

He opened his ledger. The page wrote itself.

“Subject Echo: Unregistered. Latency spiral pattern: fourfold rotation. Residue level: unstable.”
“Designation: The Silent Flame.”

The chalk reformed beneath his boots, just briefly.
A spiral, incomplete.

“She lives.”

He closed the book.
Not gently.

“And she does not yet understand what she is.”

He vanished in silence.
