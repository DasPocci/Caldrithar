🧬 Character Profile

Race: Variant Human
Class: Sorcerer (Wild Magic)
Level: 1
Background: Haunted One
Alignment: Neutral Evil
Age: 16
Height: 5’6”
Build: Curved, quietly mature
Voice: Soft. Calm. Terrifying in its restraint.

✴︎ Appearance

  • Smooth, pale skin
  • Deep brown eyes, unreadable
  • Straight dark brown hair, shoulder-length
  • Wears a dark hooded cloak, tailored black dress, and soft-soled boots
  • Carries a black leather satchel with her cult journal and components
  • No visible scars, jewelry, or identifying marks
  • Moves with elegance and silence

🧠 Personality

Personality Traits:

  • “People are tools. If they break, I find better ones.”
  • “I find beauty in destruction. It speaks plainly.”

Ideal:

  • Ascendancy — “If I was meant to hold nothing… I’ll take everything instead.”

Bond:

  • “My name is a curse. One day, I’ll teach the world to fear saying it.”

Flaw:

  • “I crave control. Over others. Over magic. Over death.”

🧪 Abilities & Skills

Ability Scores (Post-ASIs):

  • STR 8
  • DEX 13
  • CON 15
  • INT 14
  • WIS 12
  • CHA 16

Saving Throws: Constitution, Charisma
Skills: Arcana, Deception, Persuasion, Investigation, Survival
Languages: Common, Abyssal, Infernal
Passive Skills:

  • Perception: 11
  • Insight: 11
  • Investigation: 15

Feat: War Caster

🧷 Spellcasting

Spell Save DC: 13
Spell Attack Mod: +5
Sorcery Points: 0 (start at level 2)
Tides of Chaos: 1/rest
Wild Magic Surges: Triggered on GM call when casting leveled spell

Cantrips:

  • Fire Bolt
  • Prestidigitation
  • Minor Illusion
  • Light

1st-Level Spells (2 slots):

  • Chaos Bolt
  • Shield

⚔️ Equipment

  • 1 Dagger
  • Component pouch
  • Explorer’s pack
  • Hooded cloak
  • Cryptic cult journal (unreadable entries, unsettling margins)
  • Black leather satchel
  • 3 gp, 7 sp

🕯️ Magical Signatures

  • Prestidigitation effects linger too long, emit faint whispers
  • Chaos Bolt crackles in inward spirals
  • Shield shows a warped glimpse of her reflection
  • Fire Bolt ignites white-hot, leaves symbols where it lands
  • Wild Magic Surges cause taste shifts, hair to lift, and reality to hum

🕵️ Mannerisms

  • Long, unblinking eye contact
  • Speaks only after a deliberate pause
  • Never lies unless the truth would hurt less
  • Sometimes whispers to her journal when alone
  • Touches her spell pouch with her left hand before every casting

👁️ Faction Interest: Weavebound Accord

  • Watcher: Thornspeaker Vaelith, a Weave Inquisitor tracking her residual echoes
  • “The ritual did not fail. It became something we don’t understand.”
  • He refers to her as The Silent Flame — and he is watching

🗨️ Roleplay Hook (Voice Quote)

“You think I’m dangerous because of what I can do.
You should fear what I let you believe I won’t.”
