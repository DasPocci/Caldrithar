Combat Quick-Reference

Seraphyne Vexmoor

⚔️ Offense

  • Spell Attack Bonus: +5 (Charisma +3 + Proficiency +2)
  • Spell Save DC: 13
  • Dagger (Melee or Thrown): +3 to hit, 1d4+1 piercing
  • Fire Bolt (Cantrip): +5 to hit, 1d10 fire (range 120 ft)

🎭 Cantrips

  • Fire Bolt — Ranged fire attack
  • Prestidigitation — Flavor, clean, chill, spark — she often uses unsettling effects
  • Minor Illusion — 5 ft cube image/sound, INT save to disbelieve
  • Light — Touch object to glow for 1 hour (20/20 light)

🧨 Level 1 Spells

(2 slots per long rest)

  • Chaos Bolt — 2d8+1d6 damage, random energy type, may jump to another target
  • Shield (Reaction) — +5 AC until start of next turn (burns a spell slot)

🔮 Wild Magic Surge

  • Triggered when you cast a sorcerer spell of 1st level or higher. DM may call for a surge (or use Tides of Chaos).

✴︎ Tides of Chaos (Class Feature)
Once per long rest, gain advantage on one attack roll, ability check, or saving throw.
After use, DM can trigger a Wild Magic Surge to reset it.

🛡️ Defenses

  • AC: 13 (Dex +1 + base 12)
  • HP: 9 (1d6 Hit Die + CON modifier + Tough background bonus)
  • Initiative: +1
  • Speed: 30 ft
  • Saving Throws: Constitution, Charisma
  • Skills: Arcana, Persuasion, Deception, Investigation, Survival

🎒 Gear

  • Dagger ×1
  • Component pouch
  • Explorer’s pack
  • Hooded cloak
  • Cult’s cryptic journal
