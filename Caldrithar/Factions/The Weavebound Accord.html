🔹 Founding

“The gods shaped the Weave. But mortals keep tearing holes in it.”
Formed centuries ago after a series of arcane catastrophes, the Accord was founded by surviving mages of the Sundering Spiral — a ritual gone wrong that nearly erased a continent. They realized one truth: magic was too dangerous to be left to chance... or ambition.

🔹 Mission

  • Monitor forbidden magic — wild surges, ritual echoes, divine bleed
  • Hunt arcane anomalies — failed godhoods, unbound pacts, mutated sorcery
  • Contain or eliminate those who threaten the balance of the Weave

They do not prosecute crimes.
They correct errors.

✴︎ Structure

🩸 The Thornveil

The Accord's secret seat of power — a twisting, extradimensional bastion hidden inside a dead demiplane. No one enters the Thornveil unless summoned.

👁️ Thorns (Inquisitors)

Elite agents and sorceric monitors, marked by the Eye of the Thorn — a white spiral crossed by a silver thread.
They act alone, with full authority. They do not request aid.
They do not report failures — only closures.

📜 The Ledger

A living archive of magical disturbances.
Names enter it when something about them should not exist.
Seraphyne’s name is not in it.
But she triggers residue from entries long erased.

✴︎ Known Facts & Myths

  • They do not serve gods. The Accord severed its ties to divine domains to remain uncorrupted.
  • Killing a Thorn does not remove you from their lists. It adds you to another.
  • Some claim the Accord keeps a list of “future threats” — names of people who don’t yet exist.
  • Others say they have agents in disguise across mage guilds, temples, and universities.
  • Every known cult purge in the last hundred years includes Accord fingerprints.
  • Some wild sorcerers vanish when discovered. Others are found... perfectly still. No marks. No memories.
