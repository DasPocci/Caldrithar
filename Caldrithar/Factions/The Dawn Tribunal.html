✴︎ Origins

The Dawn Tribunal began as a splinter sect of the Church of Sol’reth, God of Radiant Order. Formed in secret after the Spiral Bloom Catastrophe, the Tribunal believes that some individuals are born as Weave-fractures — aberrant souls whose existence threatens the stability of magic and order.

Where Sol’reth’s clergy offers healing and redemption, the Tribunal offers eradication and silence. They see their work not as punishment, but as containment through sacrifice.

Their motto: “Burn the root, not the branch.”

Classification: Splinter Sect / Radiant Inquisition
Alignment: Lawful Evil
Headquarters: The Emberfast Bastion, a fortress-temple seared into the side of Mount Serekh

✴︎ Doctrine

  • Weave Integrity Above All — Magic must remain whole and uncorrupted.
  • Souls May Be Mistakes — Not all who live were meant to. Some are glitches in the divine design.
  • No Mercy for the Riftborn — If someone threatens the structure of reality, their destruction is salvation.

✴︎ Structure

  • Ember Judges — Elite agents, part executioner, part inquisitor. Trained in radiant and anti-chaotic magic.
  • Sunward Flames — Field operatives; cleric-acolytes trained in detection and pursuit.
  • Ash Scribes — Loremasters who catalog Weave anomalies and past ‘purges’.
  • The Dawn Mirror — A faceless council that issues absolutes. No member is seen twice.

✴︎ Resources & Influence

  • Sanctified grounds across remote mountains, deserts, and tundras
  • Ritual tools and relics that detect or suppress wild magic
  • Secret alliances with frightened cities and arcane colleges
  • Agents embedded in temples and adventuring companies as ‘watchers’

✴︎ Symbols & Rituals

  • Symbol: A stylized sun with a blackened center — the ‘sealed flame’
  • Ceremony of the Ash Sigil: Branding a Weaveborn threat before execution
  • The Purge Bell: Tolled when a fracture is confirmed — all members must fast and reflect

✴︎ Relationship to Seraphyne

The Tribunal has labeled her:

  • Codename: The Silent Flame
  • Classification: High Fracture, Class Theta
  • Directive: Terminate upon detection. Capture not advised.
  • Rationale: Ritual failure, cult resonance, persistent chaos aura

✴︎ Notable Members

  • Merek Ardyn — Ember Judge, personally assigned to hunt Seraphyne
  • Sister Virelle — Dawn Mirror seer, receives visions from the fractured Weave
  • Ashscribe Telmon — Keeper of the Purge Ledger

✴︎ Current Operations

  • Tracking cult ruins for resonance echoes
  • Offering bounties for “muted sorcerers”
  • Planting Scribes in universities and Weavebound archives
  • Preparing the Furnace Rites — a mass purge should the fractures multiply

To the Tribunal, Seraphyne is not evil. She is an anomaly. And anomalies must be sealed.
