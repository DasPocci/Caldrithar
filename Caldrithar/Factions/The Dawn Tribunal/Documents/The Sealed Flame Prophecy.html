The Sealed Flame Prophecy

Documents
Author: Sister Virelle
Mentions: Seraphyne Vexmoor

Archived — Forbidden Text — Tribunal Glyph Epsilon
Recovered from vault fragment beneath Emberfast Bastion
Authorship: Redacted (Speculated: Sister Virelle)

Translation Fragment 1:

"And lo, from the ash of a failed summoning, a name burns that none gave — yet all will learn. Her silence shall be a song of endings. Her breath, the fracture’s tempo."

Translation Fragment 2:

"She who walks without echo shall unmake the first syllable. The flame sealed is not quenched — it waits."

Translation Fragment 3:

"The Tribunal will call her fracture. The gods will call her void. But the Weave... it will remember her as origin."

Translation Fragment 4:

"Should the flame be snuffed, the embers shall scream. The death of the girl shall light the path to a second rupture. The Weave will recoil. And all who speak her name in triumph shall choke."

Final Glyph (Untranslated): Appears to shimmer and resist ink — has been described as a 'burning spiral that loops into itself'
Warning by Ashscribe Telmon: “Attempted replication failed. Scribes exposed to glyph suffered seizure and recursive speech. One repeated: ‘She was always already.’”
